# Communication Strategy

## Stakeholder Analysis

### Primary Stakeholders

**1. Government Bodies and Policymakers**
- **Needs**: Evidence-based insights for energy security policy, economic stability planning
- **Expertise Level**: Mixed - technical staff and decision-makers
- **Key Concerns**: Long-term strategic planning, risk assessment, regulatory implications
- **Preferred Format**: Policy briefs, executive summaries, technical appendices

**2. Investors and Financial Institutions**
- **Needs**: Risk assessment, investment timing, portfolio optimization
- **Expertise Level**: High - financially sophisticated
- **Key Concerns**: Short-term price movements, volatility patterns, event timing
- **Preferred Format**: Interactive dashboards, real-time data, quantitative reports

**3. Energy Companies**
- **Needs**: Operational planning, supply chain management, cost forecasting
- **Expertise Level**: High - industry experts
- **Key Concerns**: Budget planning, procurement timing, hedging strategies
- **Preferred Format**: Operational guides, scenario analysis, detailed technical reports

**4. General Public and Media**
- **Needs**: Understanding of oil price drivers, impact on daily life
- **Expertise Level**: Low to Medium - general audience
- **Key Concerns**: Gas prices, economic impact, geopolitical understanding
- **Preferred Format**: Blog posts, infographics, accessible summaries

## Communication Channels

### Written Communications

**1. Technical Reports**
- **Format**: PDF documents with executive summary, methodology, results, appendices
- **Length**: 15-30 pages for full report, 2-5 pages for executive summary
- **Audience**: Technical staff, researchers, analysts
- **Distribution**: Email, website download, academic repositories
- **Frequency**: One comprehensive final report, interim updates as needed

**2. Policy Briefs**
- **Format**: 2-4 page focused documents with key findings and recommendations
- **Structure**: Problem statement, key findings, policy implications, recommendations
- **Audience**: Policymakers, government officials
- **Distribution**: Direct email, policy forums, government briefings
- **Frequency**: Quarterly or as major findings emerge

**3. Blog Posts**
- **Format**: Medium-style articles (800-1500 words)
- **Style**: Accessible, narrative-driven, visual elements
- **Audience**: General public, media, non-technical stakeholders
- **Distribution**: Medium, company blog, LinkedIn, Twitter
- **Frequency**: Monthly or for major findings

**4. Academic Papers**
- **Format**: Peer-reviewed journal articles
- **Structure**: Abstract, introduction, literature review, methods, results, discussion
- **Audience**: Researchers, academics, technical experts
- **Distribution**: Academic journals, conferences, arXiv
- **Frequency**: As novel findings warrant

### Interactive Communications

**1. Interactive Dashboard**
- **Platform**: Web application (React frontend, Flask backend)
- **Features**: 
  - Historical price visualization
  - Event highlighting and filtering
  - Change point exploration
  - Scenario analysis tools
  - Data export functionality
- **Audience**: All stakeholders (self-service exploration)
- **Access**: Public web URL with optional authentication
- **Updates**: Real-time or near real-time data refresh

**2. Presentations**
- **Format**: Slide decks (10-20 slides for standard, 30+ for detailed)
- **Style**: Visual, minimal text, clear takeaways
- **Audience**: Stakeholder meetings, conferences, webinars
- **Delivery**: In-person, video conference, recorded webinars
- **Frequency**: As requested or for major milestones

**3. Webinars**
- **Format**: Live online presentations with Q&A
- **Duration**: 30-60 minutes
- **Audience**: Targeted stakeholder groups
- **Platform**: Zoom, Teams, or dedicated webinar platform
- **Frequency**: Quarterly or for major releases

**4. Data API**
- **Format**: RESTful API endpoints
- **Content**: Raw data, analysis results, model outputs
- **Audience**: Technical users, developers, researchers
- **Access**: API keys with rate limiting
- **Documentation**: Swagger/OpenAPI documentation

### Direct Communications

**1. Stakeholder Meetings**
- **Format**: In-person or virtual meetings
- **Duration**: 30-90 minutes
- **Audience**: Specific stakeholder groups
- **Preparation**: Tailored materials, pre-meeting briefs
- **Follow-up**: Meeting notes, action items, additional materials

**2. Office Hours**
- **Format**: Scheduled open sessions for questions
- **Duration**: Weekly or bi-weekly 1-hour slots
- **Audience**: Any interested stakeholders
- **Platform**: Video conference with optional drop-in

**3. Email Updates**
- **Format**: Periodic email newsletters
- **Content**: Key findings, upcoming events, new features
- **Audience**: Subscribed stakeholders
- **Frequency**: Monthly or quarterly

## Content Strategy by Stakeholder

## Quality Assurance

### Review Process
1. **Technical Review**: Methodology and analysis validated by technical team
2. **Domain Review**: Oil market experts validate interpretations
3. **Communication Review**: Writing and presentation reviewed for clarity
4. **Stakeholder Review**: Key stakeholders preview and provide feedback

### Accuracy Standards
- All quantitative claims backed by data with citations
- Uncertainty clearly communicated with confidence intervals
- Limitations explicitly stated
- Sources properly documented

### Clarity Standards
- Executive summaries for all technical documents
- Visual aids for complex concepts
- Glossaries for technical terminology
- Multiple formats for different audiences

## Feedback Mechanisms

### Collection Methods
- Surveys after major releases
- Comment functionality on blog posts
- Feedback forms on dashboard
- Direct email contact
- Stakeholder meeting debriefs

### Response Process
- Acknowledge feedback within 48 hours
- Address substantive feedback within 2 weeks
- Incorporate valid feedback into future iterations
- Communicate changes to stakeholders

## Crisis Communication

### Potential Issues
- Model errors or incorrect findings
- Misinterpretation by media or stakeholders
- Unexpected market events requiring rapid analysis
- Technical failures of dashboard or systems

### Response Protocol
1. **Immediate**: Acknowledge issue within 24 hours
2. **Investigation**: Determine root cause within 48 hours
3. **Correction**: Issue correction or clarification within 1 week
4. **Prevention**: Implement measures to prevent recurrence

### Spokesperson
- Designated technical lead for media inquiries
- Clear escalation path for sensitive issues
- Pre-approved messaging templates for common issues

## Measurement and Evaluation

### Metrics
- Report downloads and readership
- Dashboard usage statistics
- Stakeholder satisfaction scores
- Media mentions and citations
- Feedback quality and quantity

### Success Criteria
- Stakeholders report findings useful for decision-making
- Dashboard adopted by target users
- Analysis cited in relevant contexts
- Positive feedback from majority of stakeholders
- Minimal corrections or clarifications needed
